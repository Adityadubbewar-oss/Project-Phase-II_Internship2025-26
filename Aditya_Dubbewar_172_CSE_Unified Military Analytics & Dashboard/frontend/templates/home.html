Detailed Defense Capabilities Analysis

Defense Budget per Capita

Definition: Total Defense Budget divided by the population. It represents the average military expenditure per person.

Unit: US Dollars ($)

Significance: Acts as the baseline indicator of national military investment and defense priorities.

Power Index Rank

Definition: A comprehensive metric assessing overall military capability. It evaluates everything from personnel numbers to logistical capacity and equipment.

Unit: Index Score (Lower is better)

Significance: A score of 0 represents perfect military capability. It provides a holistic view of a nation's ability to wage conventional warfare.

Asset Distribution

Definition: The distribution of defense assets across different military branches.

Unit: Percentage (%)

Branches Overview:
- Army: Ground combat forces.
- Navy: Maritime and coastal defense.
- Air Force: Aerial superiority and support.
- Cyber: Digital and electronic warfare.
- Space: Extra-terrestrial and satellite ops.

Budget-to-GDP Ratio

Definition: The percentage of a nation's Gross Domestic Product that is allocated to its defense budget.

Unit: Percentage (%)

Significance: Indicates the economic burden of defense spending and a nation's prioritization of military capability relative to its overall economy.
