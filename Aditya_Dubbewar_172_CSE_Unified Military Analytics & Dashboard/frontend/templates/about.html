About UMACD

Unified Military Analytics and Comparison Dashboard (UMACD) is an advanced defense intelligence and analytics platform designed to transform complex military and strategic datasets into clear, interactive, and data-driven insights.

🎯 Our Mission

Mission Statement

To make global military and defense intelligence accessible, transparent, and analytically meaningful through powerful visualizations and comparative insights.

👁️ Our Vision

Vision Statement

To become a trusted analytical platform for understanding how defense capabilities, strategic investments, and technological advancements shape modern global security.

"Understanding global security begins with the ability to visualize and interpret military power clearly."

📊 The Power Matrix

Analyzing Global Defense Landscapes

A deep-dive into the structural mechanics and current state of global military capability.

Global Capability Gap

The top 10% of global powers currently capture nearly 80% of all defense capabilities, while emerging nations possess minimal force projection.

Power Index Metrics

Tracking national health through Power Index scores, where 0 represents perfect military capability and higher scores signify relative weakness.

Middle Power Squeeze

Despite rising global GDP, the middle-tier nations in many regions face budget stagnation, shrinking defense procurement, and rising security costs.

Regional Disparities

Contrasting the relative stability of OECD nations against the extreme security volatility and capability gaps in Sub-Saharan Africa and SE Asia.

The "Ladder" Analogy

Think of global power as a ladder. In a healthy society, the rungs are close enough that anyone can climb with effort. Today, however, many are finding that the rungs are moving further apart—making it harder for those at the bottom to reach the top, regardless of their hard work. UMACD visualizes these gaps to foster solutions.
